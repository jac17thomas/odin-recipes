Pizza Recipe

Description

One of the world's favorite dishes is known as the Pizza. With some melted cheese to add an addicting flavor and a crust with a crunch, the pizza hits the spot for any meal any time.

Ingredients

  • 1 roll pizza dough
  • 1 quart tomato sauce
  • 1 cup Mozarella cheese
  • 10 oz Pepperonis

Steps

  1. press pizza dough into a circle
  2. spread tomato sauce on the dough
  3. sprinkle cheese and pepperonis on top
  4. place in the oven for 25 minutes on hgh
