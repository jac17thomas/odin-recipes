Lasagna Recipe

Description

This recipe is a favorite among gluten free people and gluten eating people alike.
The noodles, excellent arrange of melted cheese and beef make for a good combo
of protein and chewy goodness.

Ingredients

  • 1 Box Lasagna Noodles
  • 1 cup beef
  • 2 cups cheese
  • 1 can tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Bring 3 cups of water to a boil
  2. Add lasagna noodles for 4 minutes until soft
  3. Place a layer of noodles in a glass pan
  4. add a layer of tomato sauce and add a noodle layer on top
  5. Repeat above until full then cook and add meat
  6. add a layer of cheese on top
