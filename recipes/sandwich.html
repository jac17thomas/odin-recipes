Sandwich Recipe

Description

A classic meal for people with no time to cook! If you are looking for a filling, quick meal go ahead and try out the sandwich. With its protein high meat and cheese you are bound to put on some muscle with this dish.

Ingredients

  • two pieces of bread
  • a block of cheese
  • mayonnaise
  • sandwich meat

Steps

  1. Take two pieces of bread and put mayonnaise on one
  2. cut a slice of cheese and place it on the bread
  3. Put two slices of meat on top of the bread and cheese
  4. put the last piece of bread on top
